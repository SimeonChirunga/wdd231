Overview

Purpose

This site serves as the official online presence for Thornhill High School—disseminating timely, official information to students, parents, and staff, while showcasing our mission, events, and achievements to prospective families.

Audience

Primary stakeholders include current students and parents looking for resources and event updates. Secondary audiences are prospective students and families evaluating school culture, academics, and extracurriculars.
