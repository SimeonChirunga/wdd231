About City of Gweru

Gweru City, strategically located in the heart of Zimbabwe, offers a unique blend of economic opportunity, rich history, and cultural diversity. As a hub for industrial and commercial activities, Gweru provides a fertile ground for investors seeking to tap into the vast potential of the African market. With its well-developed infrastructure, including a international airport, railway line, and major highways, Gweru is an ideal location for businesses looking to establish a presence in the region.

Gweru City boasts a skilled and educated workforce, with several institutions of higher learning, including the Midlands State University. This provides investors with access to a pool of talented and motivated professionals, ready to drive business growth and innovation. Additionally, the city's relatively low cost of living and doing business makes it an attractive destination for companies looking to establish a presence in Africa without breaking the bank.

The City of Gweru is committed to creating a business-friendly environment, with a range of incentives and support services available to investors. From tax breaks and investment subsidies to streamlined regulatory processes and business incubation programs, we are dedicated to helping businesses thrive. Whether you're looking to establish a manufacturing plant, retail outlet, or service-based business, Gweru City offers a unique combination of opportunity, infrastructure, and support to help you achieve your goals.
